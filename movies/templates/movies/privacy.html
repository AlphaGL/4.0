Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Watch2D (“we”, “us”, the “app” and the website at watch2d.org) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile app and website. By using Watch2D, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information — if you create an account, your email address (handled by our authentication provider). Creating an account is optional.
  • Usage data — items you watch, your watchlist, history, ratings, comments, and the genres you choose, used to personalize your experience.
  • Device & notification data — a push-notification token and basic device information so we can send you alerts about new content and community activity.
  • Diagnostics & analytics — anonymous usage statistics and crash reports that help us fix bugs and improve the app.
  • Advertising data — our ad partner may collect a device advertising identifier to show relevant ads.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and personalize the service (e.g. “Picks for you”).
  • Save your list, history, and community messages.
  • Send notifications you opt into (new releases, watch-along reminders).
  • Show advertising that keeps the app free.
  • Maintain security and improve performance.

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third parties that may process limited data under their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — accounts, database, and community features.
  • Google Firebase — push notifications, analytics, and crash reporting.
  • Start.io — in-app advertising.
  • Cloudflare — content delivery and hosting.

4. Content Disclaimer

Watch2D does not host any video files. The app and website aggregate and link to media made available by third-party sources. We are not responsible for content hosted on external services.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

We use local storage and similar technologies to remember your settings, keep you signed in, and enable offline browsing. Advertising partners may use their own cookies or identifiers.

6. Children’s Privacy

Watch2D is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will remove it.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

We keep your information only as long as needed to provide the service. Community chat messages are automatically deleted after a short period. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing us at the address below.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted on this page, with an updated “Last updated” date.
